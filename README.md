# BucketheadDiscographyChecklist
Tired of losing track of which albums you've listened to in Buckethead's discography? This website is here to help you keep track of your progress.

This is a website that saves your progress on a checklist of Buckethead's discography. All albums were automatically scraped from buckethead's wikipedia discography page and are updated periodically. You can check off albums you've listened to, and the website will save your progress in your browser's local storage.


# Usage 
you can visit the website on [https://spark4444.github.io/BucketheadDiscographyChecklist/BucketheadDiscographyChecklist/index.html](https://spark4444.github.io/BucketheadDiscographyChecklist/BucketheadDiscographyChecklist/index.html).

# Features
* Bulk buttons to clear or enable all checkboxes on the page that are currently visible.
* Search bar to filter albums by title.
* All albums are split into types (Albums, Pikes, Collaborations, etc.).
* Most albums have their name, year, length and total index of the album in Buckethead's discography.
